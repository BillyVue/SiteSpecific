WWU Imposter


For this game, you will need to take 3 pictures. Once everyone is done, get in a circle and show each other the pictures you took. The catch is, there could be imposters among the group. Not everyone will get the prompts on what to take a picture of.

If you're innocent, the prompts will be visible to you and your goal is to prevent yourself from getting accused of being an imposter, while rooting out all the imposters at the end.

If you're an imposter, your goal is to do your best to copy what you think might be the prompt and blend in with the others, while not being rooted out at the end.

When Everyone is ready, press start
