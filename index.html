WWU Imposter

For this game, you will be given 3 prompts on what to take a picture of. Once everyone is done, get in a circle and show each other the picture you took for each. The catch is, there will be imposters among the group. Not everyone will get the prompt on what to take a picture of. If you get the prompt, your goal is to prevent yourself from getting accused of being an imposter, and root out all the imposters. If you're an imposter, your goal is to do your best to copy what you think might be the prompt and blend in with the others.
